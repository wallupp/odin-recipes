A Beautiful Pizza

This amazingly beautiful pizza was created by the Chicago gods and brought down to the heavens to allow us the taste of this ultimate form of creation.

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces low moisture mozzarella cheese, thinly sliced
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ cup no salt added canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 leaves fresh basil, torn
  • Fresh Dough

Steps

  1. Make dough
  2. Put desired sauce on dough
  3. Sprinkle low moisture mozzarella on sauce
  4. Add desired toopings
